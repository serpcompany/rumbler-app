# Product Vision

**Mission**: Connect adult fighters to quality training partners and verified venues—safely, fast.

**MVP Goals**
1. Mobile-first, swipe-style discovery.
2. Identity-gated (18+) community.
3. Venue bookings + geofenced check-ins.
4. Lightweight chat and clear rulesets.

**KPIs**
- KYC pass rate
- Swipe→Match, Match→First Message, Msg→Booking conversion
- Session completion & safety rating
- Zero store-policy violations
