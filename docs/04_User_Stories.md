# User Stories & Epics

## EPIC: Profiles & Discovery
- As a fighter, I want to set my **age (DOB), gender, weight class, discipline(s), experience level (amateur/pro), and my amateur/pro records** so I get matched with fighters of comparable skill and experience.

## EPIC: Accounts & KYC
- As a new user, I want to verify my identity so only adults participate.

## EPIC: Matchmaking & Chat
- As a fighter, I want to swipe through nearby fighters to find partners.
- As a matched fighter, I want to chat to align on rules and availability.

## EPIC: Venue Bookings
- As matched fighters, we want to book a verified gym with a clear ruleset and time.

## EPIC: Safety & Moderation
- As a user, I want to report/block to keep my experience safe.
- As a moderator, I need a review queue to handle flags.
